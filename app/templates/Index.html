Ecology & Health Sustainable Platform

Ecology & Health Sustainable Platform (EHSP) generates multiple environmental parameters derived from different satellite remote sensing images through Google Earth Engine (GEE). The users only need to provide the shapefile (*.shp) of any specific study regions, which might contain administration boundary levels with a unique ID column, EHSP will automatically generate either zonal statistic summaries or raster files in the study regions upon request.
